Computer Brains: How LPUs and GPUs Power AI Differently

  • How are LPUs different from GPUs, and why does this save money?
    LPUs are like assembly lines while GPUs are like tiny factories that need to be rebuilt for each task.
    1. LPUs keep everything inside: They keep all the AI's "thoughts" (parameters) inside the chips.
    2. GPUs use outside memory: They have to constantly grab information from separate memory chips.
    3. LPUs use less energy: This makes them about 3 times more efficient.
    4. LPUs cost less: They can run AI models for about 5 times less money than GPUs.
    This matters because running big AI models is super expensive, and cheaper chips mean more people can use AI!
  • Why is keeping AI information inside chips better than using outside memory?
    Keeping information inside chips instead of using outside memory is like having everything you need right at your desk versus running to the library every few minutes!
    1. It's much faster: The AI doesn't waste time grabbing information from far away.
    2. It uses less energy: Moving data between chips uses lots of power - like shouting across a playground instead of whispering to someone next to you.
    3. It's more predictable: Everything happens on a schedule, like a train, not like unpredictable car traffic.
    4. You can connect many chips: Like building with LEGO blocks, you can connect hundreds or thousands of chips together for bigger AI.
    Future AI chips will likely try to keep more information close by instead of far away!
  • What happens if all AI chips use 3 times less energy?
    If AI chips used 3 times less energy, it would change everything about AI!
    1. More AI everywhere: AI could run in more places with limited power, like phones and small devices.
    2. Cheaper to use: Companies would pay much less to run AI, so more people could afford it.
    3. Less environmental impact: AI uses LOTS of electricity - using 3 times less would help our planet.
    4. Faster innovation: When things cost less, more people can experiment and try new ideas.
    5. Different companies might win: The companies with the most energy-efficient chips could become the new leaders.
